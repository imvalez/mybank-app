# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and give actionable feedback.
- Focus discussion on code and product outcomes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Public or private intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, or pull requests that violate this code.
